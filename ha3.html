Badminton Court

Our university sports complex offers state-of-the-art badminton courts that are perfect for both recreational and competitive play. Our courts are well-maintained and regularly cleaned to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all players.

Rules and Regulations

  • All players must wear proper athletic shoes on the court.
  • No food items and other things are allowed there
  • Court etiquette must be followed at all times, including proper attire and behavior.
  • Players must clean up after themselves and dispose of any trash in designated bins.
  • Respect other players' reservations and arrive on time for your scheduled court time.
  • Courts may be reserved for up to 2 hours at a time.
