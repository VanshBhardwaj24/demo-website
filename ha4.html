Football field - University Sports Complex

About the Basketball Court

The basketball court at our university sports complex is a state-of-the-art facility with high-quality flooring and equipment. The court is designed to meet international standards and provides a perfect playing surface for both recreational and professional players.

The court can accommodate up to 10 players per team and is equipped with all the necessary facilities, including scoreboards, benches, and ball racks. The court is open to students, faculty, and staff of the university.

Facilities

  • FIFA-regulation-sized field
  • Natural grass surface
  • Seating for up to 500 spectators
  • State-of-the-art lighting system for evening games
  • Locker rooms and shower facilities
  • Equipment rental available

Rules

Before using the football field, please be sure to read and follow the following rules:

  1. Proper footwear must be worn at all times (cleats are recommended).
  2. No smoking or tobacco products are allowed on the field.
  3. No food or drink is allowed on the field (water is permitted).
  4. No pets are allowed on the field.
  5. No alcohol or drugs are allowed on the premises.
  6. Respect other players and follow fair play rules.
  7. Report any injuries or equipment issues to staff immediately.
  8. Failure to follow these rules may result in expulsion from the field and/or loss of sports complex privileges.

Booking Information

To book the football field, please contact the sports complex reception desk during business hours or use our online booking system. Bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance and are subject to availability.

Please note that a fee may be charged for court bookings, and priority may be given to university teams and clubs.
