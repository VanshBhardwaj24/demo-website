Tennis at University Sports Complex

Indoor Tennis

Our sports complex features four indoor tennis courts that are available for use by all students and faculty members.

  • Reservations can be made up to seven days in advance.
  • Courts are available on a first-come, first-served basis for walk-ins.
  • Court fees are $20 per hour for students and $25 per hour for faculty members.
  • Courts can be reserved for singles or doubles play.
Day Hours
Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Saturday-Sunday 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Outdoor Tennis

We also have four outdoor tennis courts that are available for use during the warmer months of the year

Rules and Regulations

  • Indoor tennis is played with a standard tennis ball and racket.
  • Court reservations can be made up to a week in advance.
  • Players are responsible for providing their own equipment, including rackets and tennis balls.
  • Court fees may apply, depending on the time and day of the reservation.
  • Appropriate tennis shoes must be worn on the courts. Street shoes are not allowed.
  • No food or drinks (except water) are allowed on the courts.
  • Court etiquette must be followed at all times, including proper attire and behavior.
  • Players must clean up after themselves and dispose of any trash in designated bins.
  • Respect other players' reservations and arrive on time for your scheduled court time.
  • Courts may be reserved for up to 2 hours at a time.
