Swimming pool- University Sports Complex

Facilities

Our university sports complex boasts a 50-meter Olympic-sized swimming pool, complete with diving boards, starting blocks, and lane markers. We also have a smaller warm-up pool for beginners and children. The pool area includes showers and changing rooms for your convenience.

Rules and Regulations

  • Swimmers must wear appropriate swimwear (no street clothes or undergarments).
  • No food or drink is allowed in the pool area.
  • Running, diving, or jumping into the pool is not allowed.
  • Children under the age of 14 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Swimmers must follow the lifeguard's instructions at all times.

Hours of Operation

The swimming pool is open seven days a week during the following hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Please note that the pool may be closed for maintenance or special events. Check our website or contact us for more information.
