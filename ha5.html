Basketball Court - University Sports Complex

About the Basketball Court

The basketball court at our university sports complex is a state-of-the-art facility with high-quality flooring and equipment. The court is designed to meet international standards and provides a perfect playing surface for both recreational and professional players.

The court can accommodate up to 10 players per team and is equipped with all the necessary facilities, including scoreboards, benches, and ball racks. The court is open to students, faculty, and staff of the university.

Rules and Regulations

  • Players must wear appropriate footwear and clothing while playing.
  • No food or drinks are allowed on the court.
  • Players must follow the instructions of the court officials at all times.
  • Players must not engage in any unsportsmanlike behavior, including foul language or physical contact.
  • Players must not bring any outside equipment onto the court without prior approval from the court officials.

Booking Information

To book the basketball court, please contact the sports complex reception desk during business hours or use our online booking system. Bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance and are subject to availability.

Please note that a fee may be charged for court bookings, and priority may be given to university teams and clubs.
